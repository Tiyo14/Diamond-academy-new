Why Us

Diamond Academy is a private school that is located in a highly populated residential area in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. It is a comprehensive school composed of kindergarten, primary and secondary schools. Diamond Academy is a student-focused and result driven private school that provides broad-based learning approaches and experience at an affordable tuition fee in a highly secured and conducive learning environment. Experience the excellence of Diamond, where education meets innovation. Discover why we are the premier choice for your academic journey as the school that stands out as the top choice in Addis Ababa!
